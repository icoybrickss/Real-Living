What is Bungalow?

Bungalows originated in India, where they were designed for comfort in the hot climate. Their name comes from the Hindi word "bangla," meaning "belonging to Bengal." In the early 20th century, these charming cottages crossed the ocean and found a new home in the West, embraced by the Arts and Crafts movement for their practicality and affordability. Today, bungalows continue to hold a special place in the hearts of those who appreciate their efficient design, cozy atmosphere, and undeniable charm.
